Nike

Founded in 1964 as Blue Ribbon Sports, Nike officially became Nike, Inc. in 1971, introducing the iconic swoosh logo. The 1974 launch of the waffle-soled running shoe marked a turning point, propelling Nike into global prominence. Known for endorsing legendary athletes and collaborating with influencers, Nike's commitment to innovation and cultural impact has made it a symbol of athletic excellence and style.

New Balance

New Balance (NB) is an American sports footwear and apparel brand that was established in 1906. In 1906, William J. Riley, an Irish immigrant, founded the New Balance Arch Support Company in the Boston area, manufacturing arch supports and other accessories designed to improve shoe fit.
